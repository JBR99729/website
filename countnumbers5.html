Counting Numbers 1-5

How to complete this worksheet Look at each question. Count the pictures carefully. Then circle the correct number: 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5. Example: If you see 3 stars, circle the number 3. For nursery students, even simpler: Count the pictures. Circle the number. You can also write: Instructions for kids: Look at the pictures. Count them one by one. Circle the matching number..
