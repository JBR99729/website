Counting Numbers 1-5

How to Complete This Worksheet

Look at each question.

Count the pictures carefully.

Then circle the correct number: 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5.

Example

If you see 3 stars, circle the number 3.

Instructions for Kids

Look at the pictures.

Count them one by one.

Circle the matching number.
